About
Hello, World! I’m Austin Wells, a front-end developer passionate about creating intuitive, accessible, and visually engaging user experiences. I specialize in JavaScript and React, and I’m expanding into mobile development with React Native. My focus is on building clean, efficient, and responsive interfaces that make the web feel effortless to use.

I’m currently pursuing my degree in Web Development at South Mountain Community College (graduating May 2026). As a Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society member and former Computer Club President, I value collaboration, mentorship, and continuous learning. My projects range from personal apps to full-featured web experiences, many of which I document on my blog at syntaxandcoffee.io and portfolio site austinwells.dev.

I’m always open to connecting with developers, designers, and innovators who love building meaningful digital experiences. Let’s create something amazing together!
