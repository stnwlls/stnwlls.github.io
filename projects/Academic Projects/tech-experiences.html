"Tell Us About You and Your Technological Experiences"

Survey of Computer Information Systems - Fall '24

This assignment required me to reflect on my experiences with technology, including my first device, how my tech usage has evolved, and the role technology plays in my professional life. I also had to discuss the most innovative technology to date and identify barriers to using technology effectively.


Completing this assignment allowed me to look back on how technology has shaped my life, from my early days experimenting with devices to my current journey in web development. It reinforced the importance of adaptability in the tech world, where innovation is constant, and highlighted the ongoing challenges in ensuring equitable access to technology for all.
