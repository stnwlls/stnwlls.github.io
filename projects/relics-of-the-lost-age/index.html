Encased in an unbreakable crystalline seal, this is an original fragment of the Philosopher’s Stone — the legendary creation of the Grand Alchemists. Preserved in a vial, these tears fell from Cleopatra herself as Rome eclipsed Egypt’s final queen. The liquid never evaporates, glimmering with the sorrow of dynasties lost and beauty undiminished by time. Those who possess it are said to inherit echoes of her charm — and her cunning.

Feather from Icarus' Wing
